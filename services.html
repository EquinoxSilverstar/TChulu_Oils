Our Services



Natural Hair Care Services

At Tchulu Essential Oils, we offer a range of natural hair care services designed to promote healthy hair and scalp. Our services include:

  • Hair Growth Consultations: Our expert consultants will work with you to identify the underlying causes of hair loss and recommend a personalized hair growth plan.
  • Natural Hair Care Treatments: Our treatments include scalp massages, hair masks, and other natural therapies designed to nourish and protect your hair and scalp.
  • Hair Growth Coaching: Our coaches will work with you to develop a personalized hair growth plan, including diet and lifestyle recommendations, and provide ongoing support and guidance.
  • Natural Hair Care Products: We offer a range of natural hair care products, including essential oils, hair growth serums, and scalp treatments, designed to promote healthy hair and scalp.


Our services are designed to be safe, effective, and easy to use. We use only the highest-quality natural ingredients and our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.
