ABOUT US



Tchulu Essential Oils - Tchulu Hair Growth Essential Oils

From the roots of Nourishing Roots, Revitalizing Growth
Born from a collective curiosity about natural remedies and the timeless craft of herbal care, Tchulu Essential Oils has been dedicated to unlocking the secrets of hair nourishment since 2016. Our mission is rooted in nature's principles, where healthy growth begins from within—just as trees thrive when their roots are nurtured in fertile soil.


At Tchulu,we embrace the power of moisturizing and hydrating oils to "water" your scalp, rejuvenating it and encouraging robust hair growth .

Our commitment to natural wellness is further amplified with CBD oil, offering proven benefits to combat Scalp Folliculitis and Alopecia .

By restoring hairlines and promoting thicker, healthier hair , Tchulu Essential Oils delivers solutions that go beyond hair care—supporting beards, eyebrows, and overall scalp health .

Join us on this journey of revitalization and let nature's finest ingredients transform your confidence and care routine.
