Accessibility Statement

We are committed to making our website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We continuously work to enhance the user experience for all and apply the relevant accessibility standards.

Accessibility Features

  • Keyboard navigation for all interactive elements.
  • Alternative text for images to assist screen readers.
  • High contrast and easy-to-read fonts for better visibility.
  • Responsive design to adapt to various devices and screen sizes.
  • Clear and consistent navigation structure.

Ongoing Efforts

We are continuously working to improve accessibility. Our goal is to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA standards.

Feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on our site, please let us know. We value your feedback and aim to address your concerns promptly.

Contact us at: tchulu@gmail.com

Third-Party Content

While we strive to ensure accessibility on our website, some third-party content or plugins may not fully meet accessibility standards. We are actively working to provide accessible alternatives.
